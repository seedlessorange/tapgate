# TapGate Brand Guide

## Company one-liner

TapGate turns your phone into your gate remote. Retrofits any residential gate in about 10 minutes. Share access by text message — no fobs, no codes, no fumbling.

## What makes TapGate different

1. **Retrofit in 10 minutes** — Connects to any gate operator via trigger terminals. Two wires. Keep the gate you already own.
2. **Phone and watch, not just phone** — iPhone, Android, Apple Watch. One tap from any of them.
3. **Share by text message** — Send a guest a link, they install the app, they're in. No gate codes to remember, no fobs to hand off.
4. **Works offline** — Pure Bluetooth. No WiFi required. No monthly fees required.
5. **Optional cloud** — €5/month adds remote management, activity logs, notifications. Skip it and TapGate still works perfectly.
6. **Family-run, 25 years in access control** — Parent company heritage from a family business that's been making access control systems since 2000.

## Brand personality

- **Warm, specific, and confident.** Write like a knowledgeable friend who happens to know gates.
- **Allergic to jargon.** Explain Bluetooth and trigger terminals without sounding like a spec sheet.
- **Grounded, not hyped.** No "revolutionary," no "game-changing," no "seamless."
- **Practical over clever.** Every claim is paired with a concrete detail.
- **Personality over polish.** Lines like "Hand out keys like candy" and "Ditch the remote" are the brand voice.

## Voice & tone rules

- Second person ("you") when addressing the homeowner
- Sentence case for body copy. Title Case for headings.
- Brand name: **TapGate** — capital T, capital G, no space, no hyphen
- Use contractions — "It's," "you'll," "we've"
- Be concrete — "5 minutes," "two wires," "under 1 second," "30-metre range"

### Avoid these words

seamless, cutting-edge, revolutionary, solutions (as a noun), empower, leverage, game-changer, unlock (as metaphor), innovative, next-generation, disrupt, best-in-class

## Typography

- **Primary:** Inter (400, 500, 600, 700)
- **H1:** 56–72px desktop / 40–44px mobile
- **H2:** 40–48px
- **H3:** 24–28px
- **Body:** 16–18px
- **Letter-spacing:** -0.02em for H1/H2; 0 for body; +0.08em for uppercase eyebrows

## UI patterns

- Rounded corners: 8px inputs, 12px cards, 16px large containers, 999px pill buttons
- Subtle shadows: `0 1px 3px rgba(15, 26, 26, 0.06)`
- Buttons: primary teal pill (white text); secondary teal outline pill
- Hover: subtle lift, no color flips
- Focus: 2px teal outline, visible and accessible

## Two-brand strategy

- **TapGate** — European market, B2C, direct-to-consumer
- **ZoomIN** — US market, B2B, dealer network

These brands are visually and verbally distinct. No cross-brand mentions on the TapGate site.
