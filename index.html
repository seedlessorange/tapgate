From Box to Open Gate in 5 Minutes

01

Hook It Up

Pop open your gate operator, find the trigger terminals (same ones your remote connects to), and plug in TapGate. Two wires. That's literally it. 12V, 24V, relay-triggered... doesn't matter.

02

Grab the App

There's a QR code right on the device. Scan it, download the app, make an account, pair over Bluetooth. Maybe 90 seconds? Probably less if you're quick with passwords.

03

You're Done

Seriously, that's it. Tap the button, gate opens. Start handing out keys to whoever needs them. Wonder why you didn't do this years ago.
