Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Who we are

TapGate is a product of ISN Security Networks ("TapGate", "we", "us"). You can reach us any time at hello@tapgate.com.

What we collect

When you order a TapGate or contact us, we collect your name, email, shipping address, and any details you share about your gate. When you use the TapGate app, the device communicates with your phone over Bluetooth — by default, nothing about your gate activity leaves your phone.

If you opt into Cloud Pro, we additionally store access logs (who opened the gate, and when) on our servers for up to 90 days so you can review them in the web dashboard.

How we use it

  • To fulfill orders and ship the hardware.
  • To provide customer support and warranty service.
  • To send occasional product updates — you can unsubscribe from any email.
  • To operate Cloud Pro features if you subscribe.

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers.

Your rights

You can ask us to export, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you by emailing privacy@tapgate.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies needed to serve pages. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the date above and notify registered customers by email.
